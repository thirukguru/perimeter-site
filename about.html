About TesseriQ

Cloud security from posture to prediction.

TesseriQ builds Perimeter and Foresight for AWS-native teams that need continuous security visibility without slowing down product delivery.

What we build

Perimeter is a continuous AWS security platform for posture management, drift detection, IaC and Dockerfile scanning, compliance evidence, findings workflow, and risk intelligence.

Foresight adds predictive cloud risk intelligence for teams that want to spot likely drift and risky change patterns before they become incidents.

Why we exist

Security teams are asked to move faster with fewer blind spots, while engineering teams are asked to ship without waiting on slow review cycles. TesseriQ is designed to make cloud risk visible, explainable, and actionable for both sides.

How we work

  • Agentless AWS scanning through a cross-account, read-only role.
  • Tenant-specific ExternalId support to reduce confused-deputy risk.
  • Findings designed for remediation workflows, not just dashboards.
  • Compliance evidence and predictive risk intelligence where teams need more than point-in-time posture checks.
